Welcome To Water Service's!

Water is the most important part of human lives. Water comprises of 70% of the human body. The water that we drink ultimately helps our blood circulatory system, our digestive system, our urinary and excretory systems, and all other body functions to maintain an optimum balance. The most pertinent part of a human body is brain and 80 percent of it is water, severe dehydration may lead to mental imbalance. To function properly our human body needs 1.5 to 2 litres of pure water daily. We know how important water is for living but It is equally important that the water is pure and hygienic. Impure or unhygienic drinking water may cause number of diseases. Every year a big number of people dye due to drinking impure water. Pregnant women, Children and elderly people are more vulnerable to water-borne diseases. Safe Water Technology LLC is striving to create awareness among masses about the importance of pure water in their lives. A common perception about the bottled drinking water is that it is pure and free from all kind of harmful components but it’s just a myth. There have been numerous research reports which have proved the harmful effects of preservatives used in bottle water. Preservatives are not the only bad thing in bottled water. Dr. Sherri Mason and her team at the State University of New York Fredonia analysed 259 bottles from nine countries and found 93 per cent of the bottles showed signs of micro plastic contamination. The research was part of a global investigation into bottled water done by Orb Media.
